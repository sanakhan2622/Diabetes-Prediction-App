Diabetes Awareness

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition affecting blood sugar regulation. Types include Type 1, Type 2, and Gestational Diabetes, each requiring specific management.

Risk Factors

Obesity, family history, inactivity, and high blood pressure increase your risk. Early screening can help you take control of your health.

Warning Signs

Watch for symptoms like excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and blurred vision. Consult a doctor if you notice these signs.

Preventing & Managing Diabetes

Healthy Eating

Choose whole grains, vegetables, and lean proteins. Limit sugary drinks and processed foods to maintain stable blood sugar levels.

Active Lifestyle

Aim for 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly, like jogging or cycling, to improve insulin sensitivity and overall health.

Routine Checkups

Regularly monitor blood sugar, blood pressure, and cholesterol. Early detection can prevent complications and support effective management.
